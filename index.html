Welcome to Wairkar’s Sea Pearl Resort

Indulge in the perfect harmony of coastal serenity and contemporary comfort as you step into the enchanting realm of Wairkar’s Sea Pearl Resort. Situated in the heart of Devbag in Malvan, Maharashtra, our distinguished 3-star haven is your gateway to a world where tranquility meets exhilaration.
From the moment you set foot within our premises, a symphony of relaxation and adventure unfolds around you. The gentle caress of the ocean breeze and the rustling palm leaves create a soothing melody that accompanies you throughout your stay. Our resort is not just a destination; it's an experience that invites you to immerse yourself in the embrace of nature.

Near By Attraction

Sindhudurg Fort

Immerse yourself in history at the awe-inspiring Sindhudurg Fort. A testament to the region's rich past, this architectural marvel stands proudly against the backdrop of the Arabian Sea.

Scuba Diving

Plunge into the underwater wonderland with our scuba diving escapades. Dive deep to explore the vibrant marine life and intricate coral formations that grace the ocean's floor.

Paragliding

Take your adventure to new heights as you soar like a bird during paragliding. Feel the thrill as you glide through the air, taking in panoramic vistas that extend from the sea to the sky.

Boating

Embark on a leisurely boat ride along the shimmering waters, allowing the beauty of the coastline to unfold before your eyes. The rhythmic waves and the salty breeze create an experience that's both soothing and invigorating.
